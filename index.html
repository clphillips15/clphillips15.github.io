About Me

Growing up in the small town of Colville Washington, there was never a ton of opportunities to pursue art. I always loved creating things, but it was not until later on in high school, and in college, that I really got involved with design. I love coming up with ideas for projects, and working with my hands. In the future I hope to explore more with photography and video. Other than art I enjoy dancing, hiking, mountain biking, camping, swimming, and skiing.
